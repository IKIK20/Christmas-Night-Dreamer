What is a Christmas Night Dreamer ?

The Christmas night dreamer is a flat screen VR which allows you to have better pacifying dreams instead of the terrifying nightmares that a lot of people have.

The package includes a set of complimentary stickers, with styles varying from a Christmas Tree to Santa. Using the VR, a random magical Christmas animation will play for two or three minutes which will automatically shut down after the given time.

If you feel like being lazy and don't want to take the VR off, there's no need to for it has extra fabrics to make it as soft as a normal eye mask.
